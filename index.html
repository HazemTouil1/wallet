About Us

The developers of VeroWallet have created digital wallets that provides complete security. Everyone can use our software to store and manage cryptocurrencies with ease. We respect, appreciate, and sincerely love blockchain technology. Our priority is the decentralization of WalletSAFU's software according to the basic principles of blockchain.

User privacy and anonymity are the main postulates for us, so we do not store your private keys, backup files or any other data on VeroWallet servers. All your private data is stored only by you. Our wallet is lightweight, with a practical and intuitive interface.
